Overview

The series Hannibal features Will Graham, who possesses a remarkable ability to understand people and their minds. He is assigned to work with the brilliant and shrewd psychiatrist Hannibal to solve crimes. Over time, a complex relationship develops between them, and Will Graham tries to uncover Hannibal's true nature.
