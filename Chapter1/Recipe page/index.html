Falafel Recipe

Falafel is one of the most popular dishes in the Middle East, crispy on the outside and soft on the inside, and is usually served with tahini or in sandwiches.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups chickpeas (soaked overnight)
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 cup parsley
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp coriander
  • Salt & pepper
  • Oil for frying

Method

  1. Mix the chickpeas, onions, garlic, and parsley with the spices.
  2. Let the mixture rest for 10 minutes.
  3. Shape the mixture into small balls.
  4. Heat the oil thoroughly.
  5. Fry the falafel until golden brown.
  6. Serve it with tahini or salad.
